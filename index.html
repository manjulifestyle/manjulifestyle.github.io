About Us.

Manju's Lifestyle is a small retail store that specialises in selling unique, high-quality products related to lifestyle, such as fashion for women. The business is owned and operated by Manjula Rasineni and is known for its personalised customer service and one-of-a-kind shopping experience.

The products offered at Manju's Lifestyle are carefully curated to cater to a specific market niche and are not readily available in larger retail chains. The boutique is dedicated to providing its customers with a unique and enjoyable shopping experience, and is known for its exceptional customer service and high-quality products. The Boutique is one of the most professional best designer Boutique in Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh. We have well trained employees from Bengal, Uttar Pradesh and Andhra Pradesh. Our Boutique has a wide selection of bridal designs from which you can choose your favourite. This is the one-stop destination for all your fashion needs, run by a woman with vision and offering you the best fashion dress, whether it be for a celebration party or wedding. The personnel at the boutique are very skilled and creative, and they are aware of your demand for fashion and can meet it. We have a large selection of various fashion designs that are affordable. All ages of women are catered to by the boutique. They could be either your tiny princess or your mother or grandmother. Our boutique's skilled designers provide the unique look you want for your special day. According to your preferences and comfort, we will have your design modified.

We accept online orders (through Whatsapp / E - mail) and provide delivery services where your product reaches your doorstep safely in a short period of time.
